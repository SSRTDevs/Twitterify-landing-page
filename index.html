Why Twitterify

Every day, over 500 million tweets are posted on Twitter as users express their opinions. While some users actively post content, Twitter also has a large number of passive users who mainly read tweets and follow discussions. To understand the sentiment behind a hashtag or popular topic, users often have to sift through an average of 20 tweets, which can be a time-consuming process.


Learn More

Twitterify is a web-based application that offers real-time statistical insights for Twitter data. It has three modules that analyze trending data, threads, and user profiles.


  • Displays the summaries of the top trends on twitter across various country.
  • Displays the summary of a thread and stats related to it.
  • Displays user info to help you stalk better.
